Asking Questions

  • Asking questions to Park Ranger Watson is simple! When you open the application, a form to enter your question is waiting for you.

  • Once you have entered your question; click “Ask”, or hit enter, and Park Ranger Watson will answer you promptly.

    Using your Notebook

  • Open up your Notebook and you can see all previously saved answers.

  • Scroll through your answers as you would a normal document or webpage.

  • Satisfied with the information that you have stored in your Notebook? Email it to a friend! Just click the little paper plane.
